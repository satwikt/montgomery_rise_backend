What is MGMix? MGMix is the Montgomery Metropolitan Internet Exchange — a facility where internet service providers and large technology companies like AWS, Google, and Meta interconnect their networks locally. Having an IX node means data does not route through Atlanta or Birmingham. This has attracted data infrastructure investment to Montgomery and created demand for locally trained technology workers.
